Process

Simple, consistent, and easy to follow—so homeowners and adjusters stay aligned.

Step 01

Assess

We document source area, affected rooms, and establish a dry standard. Photos and readings start immediately.

Step 02

Stabilize & Dry

Extraction, equipment placement, and daily monitoring until materials return to pre-loss moisture levels.

Step 03

Rebuild

Once dry, we finalize scope and restore finishes to pre-loss condition—drywall, paint, flooring, trim, and more.

Documentation-first

Our files are organized to reduce back-and-forth: moisture readings, equipment days, photos, and clear scope notes.
